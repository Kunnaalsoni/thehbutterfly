Fanaah: An Epitome Of Devotion

"I love thee with a love I seemed to lose
With my lost saints -I love thee with the breath,
Smiles, tears, of all my life! -and, if God choose,
I shall but love thee better after death."

There are times when we struggle to find appropriate words in matters of the heart, and that is when we turn to the most eminent musicians, poets and authors, to convey our sentiments related to the love. Especially, isn't it true that Every heart sings a song, incomplete until another heart whispers back. Those who wish to sing always find a song.

There is certainly no dearth of love songs but only a few are soul stirring. Out of them, one is recently unleashed song 'Fanaah' by none and other than 'Antariksh': one of the finest Hindi Rock bands in India rated by MTV. Whether your love is decades-old love or a new-found love, the recently released song 'Fanaah' by 'Antariksh' captures what is so extraordinary about a profound connection named 'Love'.

Inspiration

Every single piece of art is an extension of the inspiration behind it and 'Fanaah' is no exception. 'Antariksh' was keen to experiment with some synth-pop music that could carry forward the imaginative musical legacy of Antariksh and that is how Fanaah was born, a relatable, soothing yet unsullied musical ingenuity.

Antariksh Kaleidoscope

Background

In 2012, Varun Rajput founded Antarikshon an experimental basis in collaboration with a few friends including Mridual and Gurtej. He envisioned blending Western music with contemporary Hindi music to curate music embracing vibrant Indie Rock aura and progressive yet soulful sound vibes that so far did not exist. Antariksh, quite literally means 'outer space'. It does resonate aptly with the ideology of the band members, as they did not intend to confine to musical boundaries in terms of specific musical genre. The band unveiled its first music album, 'Khoj' in 2013 and the rest is history. 'Antariksh' has been successful in carving a niche in Indie Rock music by ingeniously redefining Indie Rock music through remarkably blending authentic Rock/Pop styles of music and contemporary Hindi Music. After mesmerizing audiences with their spellbinding performing in more than 700 shows across India, Mauritius, Madagascar, UK and USA, the band has delighted its fans through releasing a new soulful love song named Fanaah.

Achievements

The band's first song, Dheere Dheere won a prestigious VIMA India awards besides being shortlisted as a top entry for VIMA Asia Awards. Their song, 'Kaahe Re' was selected as one of the top 7 out of 170 songs to be presented to a handful of noteworthy Bollywood movie directors at 'All About Music Conference:2018' besides grabbing nomination for the VIMA Asia Awards in several categories. The band was also invited for a 5 city tour by the governments of Mauritius and Madagascar in October 2019.
